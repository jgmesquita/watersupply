\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Identifies the \doxylink{class_city}{City} class by its name, id, code, demand(rate of demand or need of delivery of water) and population (total population). 

Portrays a \doxylink{class_reservoir}{Reservoir}, identified it by name, municipality where it is located, id, its max\+\_\+delivery(maximum delivery of water) and code.
